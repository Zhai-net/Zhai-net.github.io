Research

Research Areas

△

Experimental Fluid Mechanics

High-speed transient-flow experiments using schlieren imaging, high-speed photography, and quantitative flow diagnostics.

≈

Interfacial Instability

Interface evolution driven by shocks and rarefaction waves, including perturbation growth, vorticity deposition, and mixing-layer development.

↯

Shock Dynamics

Shock generation, reflection, refraction, interference, and interactions with vortices and interfaces.

▥

Shock–Particle-Curtain Interaction

Wave evolution, particle motion, and gas–solid coupling when shocks pass through particle curtains.
